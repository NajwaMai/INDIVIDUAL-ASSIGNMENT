How I Plan to Become a Graphic Designer

  1. Complete a degree in Multimedia or Graphic Design
  2. Learn industry-standard tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma
  3. Build a strong online portfolio
  4. Gain real-world experience through internships
  5. Take freelance projects to expand my skills
  6. Join design communities and attend workshops
  7. Apply for full-time design positions
